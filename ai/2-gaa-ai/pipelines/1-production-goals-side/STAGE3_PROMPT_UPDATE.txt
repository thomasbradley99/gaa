UPDATED STAGE 3 EVENT CLASSIFICATION PROMPT
===========================================

Focus on extracting ONLY these 10 detectable GAA events:

HIGH PRIORITY EVENTS:
---------------------
**SHOTS:**
- Shot Own (home team shoots)
- Shot Opp (away team shoots)
  Tags: [From Play/From Free] + [Point/Goal/Wide]
  
**KICKOUTS:**
- Kickout Own (home keeper restart)
- Kickout Opp (away keeper restart)
  Tags: [Long/Mid/Short] + [Left/Centre/Right] + [Won/Lost]

MEDIUM PRIORITY EVENTS:
-----------------------
**TURNOVERS:**
- Turnover Won (home wins possession)
- Turnover lost (home loses possession)
  Tags: [Forced/Unforced] [middle third] (optional)

**FOULS:**
- Foul Awarded (TO home team)
- Foul Conceded (BY home team)
- Scoreable Foul Awarded
- Scoreable Foul Conceded
  No tags needed

KEY CHANGES FROM OLD PROMPT:
- Remove: Possession Own/Opp, Attack Own/OPP, Ball in Play, Stoppage, Highlight, Hot Ball
- Keep ONLY: Shot, Kickout, Turnover, Foul events
- Every Shot MUST have outcome (Point/Goal/Wide)
- Every Kickout MUST have Won/Lost outcome
